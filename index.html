30-second summary

ARENIX.app is a technology infrastructure for Partners, creators and community leaders who want to turn a passive audience into an active and measurable community.

A Partner creates their own Arena, invites members of their community, sets rules, and monitors activity, ranking and community performance in one professional environment.

The essence of the Arena is the rules. A Partner may set a model in which success in the Arena does not disappear without result, but is reflected back to the best-ranked part of the community according to a predefined key – for example to the Top 40% of the leaderboard.

In the basic public layer, this model is displayed exclusively through Arena Credits. Arena Credits are an internal platform element, have no monetary value, are not redeemable and must not be used as money or a substitute for money.

The Partner Monetization Layer is a separate and limited step for approved Partners who demonstrate real activity, discipline and commercial potential in their community. It is not an automatic part of the public platform and is governed by separate terms.

5The Arena Key

Each Arena may have its own rule key.

This key determines how results, ranking, performance and allocation of Arena Credits are displayed within the Arena.

A Partner may set rules so that Arena Credits are allocated according to the leaderboard, performance score, activity or other parameters available in the platform.

An example of such a rule may be a model under which the best-ranked part of the leaderboard, for example the Top 40%, receives back or additionally receives Arena Credits within the Arena according to a predefined key.

The Arena Key is the reason why an Arena is not merely a results table.

Participants see that rules have consequences, ranking has meaning and community activity is reflected in a visible result.

The Partner can therefore show their community a model in which performance, discipline and participation are not empty numbers, but create a structured community mechanism.

In the basic public layer, the Arena Key relates exclusively to Arena Credits.
